            <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-4">About Dragon Ball Z Supersonic Warriors</h2>

            <p class="mb-4">Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors is a 2D fighting game developed by Arc System Works and published by Atari and Banpresto for the Game Boy Advance in 2004. The game was later ported to the Nintendo DS as Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors 2 with enhanced features and additional characters.</p>

            <p class="mb-4">The game features a unique "Supersonic" battle system that allows players to fly freely around the screen, engage in high-speed combat, and unleash devastating special attacks. Each character has their own story mode that presents an alternate version of the Dragon Ball Z storyline, giving players the chance to experience "what if" scenarios.</p>

            <p>What sets Supersonic Warriors apart from other fighting games is its accessibility combined with strategic depth. The simplified control scheme makes it easy for newcomers to pick up and play, while the team battle system and various combat mechanics provide enough complexity to satisfy experienced players.</p>
