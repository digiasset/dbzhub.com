Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors

Experience the ultimate Dragon Ball Z fighting game with your favorite characters in this classic handheld title, now available to play online!

About the Game

Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors is a fighting game that brings the intense battles of the Dragon Ball Z universe to life. Originally released for the Game Boy Advance, this game features:

  • Multiple playable characters from the Dragon Ball Z series
  • Fast-paced combat with signature moves and transformations
  • Story mode following the main Dragon Ball Z sagas
  • Special attacks and ultimate techniques

Game Features

  • Authentic Dragon Ball Z fighting experience
  • Character-specific special moves and combos
  • Multiple game modes including story and versus
  • Power-up system and character transformations
  • Mobile-friendly controls for on-the-go gaming

How to Play

Playing Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors is simple:

  1. Click the game window to start
  2. Use your keyboard or touch controls to move and fight
  3. Master special moves and combos
  4. Progress through the story mode or challenge friends

Tips & Tricks

  • Learn character-specific combos for maximum damage
  • Time your transformations for strategic advantage
  • Practice blocking and dodging to avoid damage
  • Use special moves when your energy is full
  • Experiment with different characters to find your favorite
